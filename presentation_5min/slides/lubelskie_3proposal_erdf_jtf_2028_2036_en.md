# Lubelskie (PL81) ERDF + JTF Program Proposals
# 2028–2036 assumed funding window

## Slide 1: Package 1 — Energy-Poverty Reduction and Carbon-Ready Housing
- ERDF (2028–2032): retrofit public buildings and old housing, heat-pump pilots, smart metering, and SME energy-efficiency support.
- JTF (2030–2034, 2036 scale-up): retraining and reallocation support for workers in maintenance, heating, and building-services jobs.
- Scale (indicative): ERDF EUR 28–35m equivalent / JTF EUR 14–20m.
- Impact: 12,000 dwellings targeted, 10–15% lower winter energy burden, 2,500 workers re-skilled.

## Slide 2: Package 2 — Low-Carbon Mobility and Eastern Corridor Connectivity
- ERDF (2028–2034): bus fleet renewal (clean mini/bus systems), low-emission intercity links, ITS + ticket integration, rural mobility nodes.
- JTF (2029–2036): transport-mechanic and EV maintenance re-skilling, operator transition support, local supplier reorientation.
- Scale (indicative): ERDF EUR 45–60m / JTF EUR 14–26m.
- Impact: public-transport share +8–12 pp, average commuting time –8 to 12 minutes, 60% post-training employment in 6 months.

## Slide 3: Package 3 — Rural Diversification and Just-Transition Industrial Value Chain
- ERDF (2029–2036): rural agri-bio value-chain infrastructure, low-carbon logistics nodes, and SME digital/energy transition support.
- JTF (2031–2036): worker transition platform for former coal-linked and low-wage manual occupations; social support + placement links.
- Scale (indicative): ERDF EUR 40–57m / JTF EUR 16–24m.
- Impact: 40 new low-carbon SMEs, regional average employment resilience improvement, 4,000 vulnerable workers retained or re-employed.
