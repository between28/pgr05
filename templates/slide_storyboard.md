# 5-Minute Slide Storyboard Template

## Slide 1. Problem and One-Line Proposal
- Place context (where + why now)
- Policy problem in one sentence
- One-line proposal

## Slide 2. Intervention Design
- What is funded
- Who is targeted
- How delivery works

## Slide 3. Evidence Base
- 2-3 strongest evidence points
- Why effects are plausible in this place

## Slide 4. Funding Architecture
- ERDF role
- JTF role
- No-overlap implementation rule

## Slide 5. Outcomes and Ask
- Expected outcomes (equity + climate + just transition)
- Risks and mitigation
- Funding decision ask
